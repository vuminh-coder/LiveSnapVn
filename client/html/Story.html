Golden Hour at the Marina

The light hit the water just right, creating a shimmering path of gold. It was quiet, save for the gentle lapping of waves against the dock.

We sat there for nearly an hour, watching the sky shift from brilliant orange to a deep, bruising purple. It felt like the world had paused just for us—a perfect moment frozen in time amidst the chaos of the city.
